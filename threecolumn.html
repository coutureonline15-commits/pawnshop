About Jay's Pawnshop

Jay's Pawnshop has served the community since 2002. We buy, sell, and loan on jewelry, electronics, tools, collectibles, and more. Our goal is simple: fair deals, transparent pricing, and reliable service.

Store Policy

Layaway: 20% down. Balance due within 60 days.

Returns: All sales are final. Electronics and tools include a 7-day functionality guarantee.

Loans: Terms follow state law. Timely payment required to retain pawned items.

ID: Valid government photo ID required for pawn transactions.
