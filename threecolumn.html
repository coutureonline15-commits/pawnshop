About Jay's Pawnshop

Jay's Pawnshop has been serving the community since 2002. We specialize in buying, selling, and loaning on quality items such as jewelry, electronics, tools, collectibles, and more. Our mission is to provide fair deals, transparent pricing, and trustworthy service to every customer who walks through our doors or shops online.

Store Policy

Layaway: We offer layaway options on select items with a 20% down payment. Items must be paid off within 60 days.

Returns: All sales are final. Electronics and tools are tested before sale and come with a 7-day functionality guarantee.

Loans: Loan terms are set according to state regulations. Payments and extensions must be made on time to avoid forfeiture of pawned items.

Identification: A valid government-issued ID is required for all pawn transactions.
