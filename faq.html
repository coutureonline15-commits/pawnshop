Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long do I have to repay a pawn loan?
    All loans must be repaid within 30 days.
  • What is the maximum loan amount?
    Up to $10,000, depending on item value.
  • How much can I borrow against an item?
    We loan up to 25% of the item’s appraised value.
  • What happens if I don’t repay?
    The pawned item becomes shop property after the loan term ends.
  • Do you buy items outright?
    Yes, we purchase gold, jewelry, electronics, tools, and collectibles.
