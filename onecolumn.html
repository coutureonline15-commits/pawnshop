Our Services

At Jay’s Pawnshop, we provide fast, fair, and reliable services to meet your financial and shopping needs. Whether you need quick cash, want to sell unwanted items, or are searching for unique treasures at affordable prices—we’ve got you covered.

Pawn Loans

Need cash today? Bring in your valuables—such as jewelry, electronics, tools, or collectibles—and receive a confidential loan with no credit check. Your items are safely stored until you’re ready to redeem them.

Buy & Sell

We buy gold, silver, electronics, tools, and more at competitive rates. Looking to shop? Our store is stocked daily with quality merchandise at prices far below retail.

Appraisals

Our experts provide on-the-spot evaluations of jewelry, precious metals, and other valuables. You’ll always get a fair and honest appraisal—whether you’re pawning, selling, or just curious.

Layaway Program

Found something you love but want to pay over time? Ask about our layaway options—secure your item today with flexible payment plans that work for your budget.

Special Deals

We proudly offer discounts for military members, first responders, and loyal repeat customers. Ask our staff about current promotions and seasonal specials.
